# Summerset Isles — Adventure Research

## 1. Lore Overview

The Summerset Isles is an archipelago of fourteen islands located southwest of Tamriel, west of Valenwood. It is the ancestral homeland of the Altmer (High Elves), the oldest and most magically advanced civilization on Nirn. The province's identity is defined by arcane mastery, rigid social hierarchy, breathtaking architecture, and an unwavering pursuit of perfection inherited from the Aldmer.

### Geography

The archipelago consists of three major islands:

- **Summerset (Isle of Summerset)** — The largest island, featuring pink flowering trees, vibrant hills, stony mountains, and rocky-grassy coastlines. Home to the Crystal Tower atop Eton Nir (the highest peak), the capital Alinor, and most of the population.
- **Auridon** — The second largest island, gateway to Summerset from the mainland. More rocky and sandy beaches. Major cities: Firsthold, Skywatch, Vulkhel Guard.
- **Artaeum** — Third largest island, home to the Psijic Order. Known for mysteriously vanishing from Nirn for centuries at a time, disappearing at least twice. Houses the ancient Ceporah Tower, which predates even Aldmeri settlement.

## 3. Races & Peoples

### Altmer (High Elves)
- Descendants of the Aldmer who sailed from Aldmeris
- Longest-lived mortal race, with lifespans of centuries
- Caste-based society with rigid social hierarchy (the Ceremoniarchy)
- Supreme mastery of magic — considered the finest spellcasters on Nirn
- Culturally value perfection, tradition, and purity of bloodline
- Divine pantheon: Auri-El (chief), Trinimac, Magnus, Syrabane, Y'ffre, Xarxes, Mara, Stendarr, Phynaster

### Maormer (Sea Elves)
- Exiled Aldmer who settled Pyandonea ("Veil of Mist")
- Led by the immortal King Orgnum, a deathless wizard who grows younger over time
- Relentlessly invaded Summerset throughout First and Second Eras
- Master storm magic and command sea serpents

### Sload
- Slug-like amphibious race from the coral kingdom of Thras
- Master necromancers who conjured sea monsters against Summerset
- May have inhabited Summerset before the Aldmer (ancient coral ruins)
- Released the Thrassian Plague in 1E 2200

## 6. Signature Cultural Elements

- **Crystal Tower (Crystal-Like-Law)** — Sacred monument, Stone of "Transparent Law" that mystically protects all of Summerset. Looking upon the Heart of Transparent Law awakens a dark urge to seize it. Control could grant omnipresence across the Aurbis.
- **The Ceremoniarchy** — Rigid governance system; heirs study 3,555 days of Regal Praxis before coronation. Scrolls of Praxis ordain succession.
- **Magical Supremacy** — Altmer consider themselves the finest mages in Tamriel. Ward magic, destruction, conjuration, and mysticism are signature schools.
- **Psijic Mysticism ("The Old Ways")** — Ancient discipline predating conventional magic. Practitioners manipulate the "Eleven Forces" and Space/Life.
- **Welkynar Tradition** — Initiates find wild gryphon eggs, raise hatchlings, and undergo magical bonding rituals. Rider and gryphon share thoughts and feelings.
- **Architectural Perfection** — Coral structures (pre-Aldmer, possibly Sload-built) and soaring crystalline towers. Beauty is considered a reflection of divine heritage.

## 11. Recommendation

**Build first: "Shadows over Crystal Tower"**

Rationale: the 57-card Daedra pool and deep Atronach roster build 10 distinct encounters with zero imports, and Supreme Atromancer into Dremora Markynaz delivers a climactic two-legendary finale that differs cleanly from Ancano's spell-damage identity.

**Build second: "The Unforeseen Queen"**

Mankar Camoran plus the Jaws of Oblivion Invade mechanics give the Veiled Queen's Daedric pact a unique late-game shape, and Heritance variants of existing High Elf cards produce a full slate without overlap with the Thalmor Agent deck from Eye of Magnus.

**Build third: "The King of Worms"**

Mannimarco is already a ready-made boss, and the Vastarie / Odirniran / Blood Magic Lord mini-bosses step cleanly up to his summon-from-discard finale.

**Save for later: "War of the Isle"**

Playable today using AGI serpent/Reptile enemies as Maormer stand-ins, but thematic authenticity will dramatically improve once canonical Maormer and Sea Serpent cards are imported.
