# Skyrim Adventure Research

## Lore Overview

Skyrim is the northernmost province of Tamriel, a harsh and frozen land of towering mountains, ancient Nordic ruins, and fierce warrior culture. Home to the Nords, Skyrim is divided into nine holds, each governed by a jarl. The province is defined by its Viking-inspired culture, reverence for the Thu'um (Voice), worship of the Nine Divines (particularly Talos), and a long history of conflict spanning the Dragon War, the Great War, and the Stormcloak Rebellion.

The events of TES V: Skyrim (4E 201) define the province's most recent era: the return of Alduin and the dragons, the Stormcloak Civil War, the machinations of the Thalmor, and numerous guild questlines including the Companions, Thieves Guild, Dark Brotherhood, and College of Winterhold.

## Races & Peoples

- **Nords** — Dominant race, warrior culture, reverence for Ysgramor and Sovngarde, mastery of the Thu'um among select few
- **Imperials** — Significant military/political presence via the Empire and Imperial Legion
- **Dunmer** — Refugee community in Windhelm's Gray Quarter after the Red Year
- **Argonians** — Dockworker communities, marginalized in cities
- **Bretons/Reachmen** — Native Reach population, including the Forsworn insurgency
- **Khajiit** — Trade caravans outside city walls
- **Altmer/Thalmor** — Political operatives enforcing the White-Gold Concordat

## Signature Cultural Elements

- **Thu'um / Dragon Shouts** — The Voice, taught by the Greybeards, central to the Dragonborn identity. Cards with "Shout" rules tag exist (Unrelenting Force, Call of Valor, Fire Breath, Drain Vitality, Soul Tear)
- **Sovngarde** — Nord afterlife for the honored dead, accessible in the main quest
- **Companions / Mead Hall Culture** — Jorrvaskr, shield-siblings, honor duels
- **Skyforge Steel** — Legendary forge at Whiterun, weapon excellence
- **Nightingales** — Secret Thieves Guild elite serving Nocturnal
- **Dawnguard** — Ancient vampire-hunting order, crossbows and trolls
- **Civil War** — Nord nationalism vs Imperial unity, Talos worship ban

## Adventure Proposals

### Proposal 1: Shadows Over Riften (Thieves Guild / Nightingales)

#### Adventure Header
- **Name:** Shadows Over Riften
- **Setting:** Riften -> Ratway -> Goldenglow Estate -> Irkngthand -> Twilight Sepulcher
- **Storyline:** The Thieves Guild is in decline, its luck stolen along with the Skeleton Key from the Twilight Sepulcher. As you rise through the Guild's ranks, you discover that Guildmaster Mercer Frey has betrayed the Nightingales and Nocturnal herself. Recover the Skeleton Key and restore the Guild's fortune.

#### Enemy Decks

#### 1. Ratway Cutthroats (Combat)
- **Focus:** `agi_helstrom_footpad` *Helstrom Footpad* (AGI, 2, 3/2) — cheap AGI thug that pressures lanes early.
- **Supporting:** `hos_agi_palace_prowler` *Palace Prowler*, `hos_agi_caravan_enforcer` *Caravan Enforcer*, `hos_agi_lockpick` *Lockpick*, `agi_thieves_guild_recruit` *Thieves Guild Recruit*
- **Modifier:** *New mechanic:* Cramped Sewers — each lane has capacity 2 this fight; extra summons fizzle.

#### 2. Goldenglow Mercenaries (Combat)
- **Focus:** `wil_kvatch_soldier` *Kvatch Soldier* (WIL, 2, 2/3) — Guard infantry that walls up the bee estate's approach.
- **Supporting:** `wil_imperial_legionnaire` *Imperial Legionnaire*, `wil_septim_guardsman` *Septim Guardsman*, `hos_wil_solitude_stalwart` *Solitude Stalwart*, `hos_wil_imperial_camp` *Imperial Camp*
- **Modifier:** Hive Lane (field) — enemy creatures summoned there gain +0/+1 Guard (hired guards bunker in).

#### 3. Riften Pickpockets (Combat)
- **Focus:** `hos_int_riften_pickpocket` *Riften Pickpocket* (INT, 4, 2/2) — Summon peels cards out of your deck.
- **Supporting:** `hos_agi_barter` *Barter*, `hos_int_thief_of_dreams` *Thief of Dreams*, `hos_wil_thieves_guild_shadowfoot` *Thieves Guild Shadowfoot*, `hos_agi_paralyze` *Paralyze*
- **Modifier:** Pilfer — whenever an enemy creature damages you through an empty lane, the enemy draws a card.

#### 4. Maven's Enforcers (Combat)
- **Focus:** `hos_wil_bandit_ringleader` *Bandit Ringleader* (WIL, 6, 3/5) — grants lanemates Pilfer-draw the turn he lands.
- **Supporting:** `wil_bruma_profiteer` *Bruma Profiteer*, `wil_skingrad_patroller` *Skingrad Patroller*, `hos_agi_nightprowler` *Nightprowler*, `hos_agi_embassy_disguise` *Embassy Disguise*
- **Modifier:** Guard — Maven's thugs always summon the first creature each turn with temporary Guard.

#### 5. Silver-Blood Reachmen (Combat)
- **Focus:** `hos_neu_forsworn_looter` *Forsworn Looter* (NEU, 2, 1/1) — Pilfer draws a cost-matching item from the deck.
- **Supporting:** `str_battlerage_orc` *Battlerage Orc*, `str_rampaging_minotaur` *Rampaging Minotaur*, `hos_str_battlefield_scrounger` *Battlefield Scrounger*, `hos_neu_horned_helm` *Horned Helm*
- **Modifier:** *New mechanic:* Markarth Mines — each turn the enemy puts a random 2-cost item into its hand.

#### 6. Falmer of the Ratway Vaults (Combat)
- **Focus:** `hos_agi_nightprowler` *Nightprowler* (AGI, 6, 5/3) — draws on gaining Cover, snowballs in tunnels.
- **Supporting:** `hos_agi_gloomlurker` *Gloomlurker*, `hos_agi_shadowmaster` *Shadowmaster*, `hos_agi_sightless_skulk` *Sightless Skulk*, `hos_agi_deepwood_trapper` *Deepwood Trapper*
- **Modifier:** Shadow lane (shadow) — enemy creatures summoned here gain Cover at end of turn.

#### 7. Dwemer Sentries of Irkngthand (Combat)
- **Focus:** `neu_dwarven_sphere` *Dwarven Sphere* (NEU, 3, 2/3) — Summon Shackles one of your lanes.
- **Supporting:** `neu_spider_worker` *Spider Worker*, `neu_dwarven_ballista` *Dwarven Ballista*, `hos_neu_blackreach_rebuilder` *Blackreach Rebuilder*, `hos_neu_dwarven_dynamo` *Dwarven Dynamo*
- **Modifier:** *New mechanic:* Steam Pipes — at the start of the enemy's turn, one random enemy creature gains +1/+1.

#### 8. Nightingale Betrayers (Combat)
- **Focus:** `agi_quinrawl_burglar` *Quin'rawl Burglar* (AGI, 6, 4/4) — Drain + Pilfer: +4/+4 snowballs the end-game.
- **Supporting:** `hos_agi_serpentine_stalker` *Serpentine Stalker*, `hos_agi_shadowmaster` *Shadowmaster*, `hos_agi_shadowscale_partisan` *Shadowscale Partisan*, `hos_dual_assassins_ritual` *Assassin's Ritual*
- **Modifier:** Consume — at the end of each enemy turn, the enemy sacrifices its weakest creature and another gains +2/+2.

#### 9. Karliah, Nightingale Scorned (Mini-Boss)
- **Focus:** `hos_agi_thieves_guild_fence` *Thieves Guild Fence* (AGI, 3, 3/2, legendary) — every card the enemy draws on its turn costs 1 less.
- **Supporting:** `hos_agi_swims_at_night` *Swims-at-Night*, `agi_brotherhood_assassin` *Brotherhood Assassin*, `hos_int_riften_pickpocket` *Riften Pickpocket*, `hos_agi_paralyze` *Paralyze*
- **Modifier:** *New mechanic:* Twilight Sepulcher — enemy's first Pilfer each turn triggers a bonus 1 damage to the player.

#### 10. Mercer Frey, Skeleton Key Thief (Boss)
- **Focus:** `hos_agi_brynjolf` *Brynjolf* (AGI, 5, 4/5, legendary) — stand-in face for Mercer; Drain plus Pilfer/Drain magicka ramp.
- **Supporting:** `agi_quinrawl_burglar` *Quin'rawl Burglar*, `hos_agi_ring_of_namira` *Ring of Namira*, `dg_agi_zephyr` *Zephyr*, `hos_agi_serpentine_stalker` *Serpentine Stalker*
- **Modifier:** *New mechanic:* Skeleton Key — at the start of the boss's turn, a random friendly enemy creature gains +0/+2 and Guard, representing Mercer unlocking its defenses.

#### Adventure Nodes
- **Boons:** "Nocturnal's Favor", "Luck of the Guild"
- **Events:** "The Goldenglow Job" — sneak (grant Cover to all creatures next combat) or burn (3 damage to all enemies next combat, -5 max HP).
- **Shop/Reinforcement:** "The Ragged Flagon" (shop), "Nightingale Recruit" (reinforcement)
- **Augment nodes:** "Nocturnal's Blessing" (creature augment), "Pickpocket's Haul" (action augment)

#### Feasibility
- Pilfer and AGI enemy pool is deep (45+ cards). Mercer Frey boss uses Brynjolf as mechanical face since no Mercer card exists. All other references verified in `card_catalog.gd`.

## Recommendation

**Build first: Tyranny of the Sun (Dawnguard)** — Lord Harkon's built-in transformation chain gives the boss a natural escalation, and the vampire/undead card pool is uniquely deep for a varied 10-encounter arc.

**Build second: Forbidden Knowledge (Dragonborn DLC)** — Miraak is a ready legendary and the Daedra/Dragon/Cultist pools fill three distinct fight zones (Solstheim, Apocrypha, Dragon temples).
